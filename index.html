Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20th February 2026

Daily Bible Verses – Worthy (“Worthy,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

By using Worthy, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Subscription Information

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. When you purchase a subscription or lifetime access, the App Store provides transaction and subscription status information. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

B. Anonymous Usage Analytics

We use Supabase to collect limited, anonymous analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. This may include:

  • Feature usage
  • Screen views
  • App interaction events
  • Subscription status events (e.g., started trial, active, cancelled)

This analytics data is intended to be non-identifying and does not include your name or personal identity.

C. Age Range (Aggregated Only)

During onboarding you may select an age range. We use this to understand aggregate distribution (counts by age range) to improve the app. We do not store your age range in a personal profile tied to you.

D. Crash Reports and Diagnostics

We may use Sentry to collect crash reports and technical diagnostics to improve stability. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Error logs

This information is used only to troubleshoot issues and improve performance.

E. Content Stored on Your Device

Journal entries, saved verses, streaks, preferences, and your chosen display name (if you enter one) are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your journal content or display name on our servers.

Note: If you have iCloud device backup enabled, iOS may include app data as part of your device backup. This is controlled by Apple’s system settings and is separate from any in-app syncing.

F. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may send reminders or encouragement messages. Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or require:

  • Account login credentials
  • Contacts
  • Photos or camera data
  • Precise location
  • Sex / gender information
  • Payment card details (handled by Apple)

Advertising Tracking

We do not currently use advertising tracking (such as IDFA-based tracking) or session recordings. If we add advertising measurement in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, request permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide subscription access
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Monitor performance and fix bugs
  • Understand aggregated onboarding selections (such as age range counts)

We do not sell your data.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data with service providers only to operate and improve the app:

  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Supabase (anonymous analytics / aggregated metrics)
  • Sentry (crash reporting, if enabled)
  • Apple App Store (payment processing and purchase history)

5. Data Retention

  • Journal entries and local preferences remain on your device unless you delete the app.
  • Anonymous analytics and diagnostics are retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes.
  • If you contact support and request deletion of server-stored analytics related to your device, we will take reasonable steps to comply.

6. Your Choices

  • Disable notifications in your device settings.
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored data.
  • Contact us to request deletion of server-stored analytics where applicable.

7. Children’s Privacy

Worthy is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
